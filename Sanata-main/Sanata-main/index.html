Introducing Sanata

Sanata is a protocol that ensures strong data durability in a decentralized, permissionless environment. It utilizes fountain code to transform data into an endless sequence of encoded fragments, enabling robustness against data loss.

01

Key Benefits of Sanata Protocol

Durable

Offers close-to-ideal mean-time-to-data-loss (MTTDL) with minimal storage redundancy.

Decentralized

Fully decentralized process for encoding generation and storage node selection.

Secure

Designed to withstand dynamic Byzantine behaviors and targeted attacks through random selection of an infinite sequence of storage nodes.

Scalable

Efficiently scales up to over 10,000 nodes and maintains high performance levels.

02

Technical innovation that drives Sanata Protocol

Sanata employs the rateless feature of fountain codes for data encoding, coupled with a random selection mechanism for an infinite sequence of storage nodes. This design ensures data preservation even amid unpredictable and malicious network conditions and achieves impressive durability and scalability.
